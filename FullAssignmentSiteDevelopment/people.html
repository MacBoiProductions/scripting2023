People

As of 2021, Airdrie has a population of 74,100, according to Statistics Canada. The most prominent age group in Airdrie is 35 to 39.

In addition, due to Airdrie being a good place to start a family, over 50% of the residents there are families, according to Statistics Canada. The neighbourhoods are designed to be very suburban and modern for families to feel safe and comfortable.

According to Maverick Group, Airdrie is safer than 74% of the rest of Canada due to it effectively managing its crime rates very thoroughly.

There are also lots of community events to help connect Airdrie residents together. There is a local Airdrie Farmers Market hosted every Wednesday in Jensen Park, which helps give friendly, local businesses a chance to grow in the community, mostly by selling fresh produce. The farmer's market even has a donut food truck! In addition, although cancelled in recent years, Airdrie used to host a festival called ARTember, where artists could come together and create various forms of art to have on display and connect with other artists.
