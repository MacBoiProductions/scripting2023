Attractions

There are many attractions available in Airdrie both residents and visitors alike can enjoy!

Nose Creek Regional Park

Nose Creek Regional Park is a 20-acre park, sprawling from the downtown core. It contains a lush pathway around a lake with many bridges. In addition, it has an amphitheatre that sometimes holds public performances during events. As a part of Airdrie's partnership with Gwacheon, there were also four totem poles themed after the city. However, they were unfortunately removed in October 2022 due to them being deemed a public health hazard due to rotting. They will most likely be reinstated in the ground sometime in the near future. The park is often used for big citywide events, such as the Airdrie Festival of Lights during Christmas and the Airdrie Pride Parade.

The Airdrie Festival of Lights is an annual festival held and Nose Creek Park yearly during the month of Christmas, where the entire park is decorated with bright, pretty Christmas lights. The Airdrie Pride Parade, also held in Nose Creek Park, is an annual LGBTQ+ event held in June where there is a march down main street into the park where there are many activities, food trucks, and even a drag show in the amphitheatre to enjoy!

Chinook Winds Regional Park

Chinook Winds contains over 55 acres of farmland and includes many activities, mostly geared at kids, such as a skate park, a spray park, a toboggan hill, several playgrounds, amongst other things. There's even a cross country ski trail available during the winter!

Learn More!

Genesis Place

Genesis Place is a huge sports and entertainment building, meant for the whole family to enjoy! Its main attraction is the big swimming pool, containing a big slide, diving board, aquatic lanes, and so much more! There is also a hockey rink and several gymnasiums. Many sports competitions for people of all ages are held at Genesis Place. Also, after a tough workout, you can grab some Booster Juice to cool down on your way out!

Learn More!

Shamrock Lanes & Trophies

Shamrock Lanes & Trophies is a small bowling place across the street from Genesis Place. You can go to celebrate a special occasion, and duke it out with family and friends and snack on some delicious pizza! There are also bowling leagues available at Shamrock various people of all ages can join and participate in.
