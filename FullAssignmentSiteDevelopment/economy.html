Economy

Airdrie's population is expected to bloom by 38% by 2033, making it the perfect place for new businesses to grow. There are various factors at play一between Alberta's lower tax rate, as well as general growth means businesses save money if they decided to locate themselves to Airdrie.

Airdrie is a “bedroom community,” since it's become very popular to work in Calgary while living there. This is due to the fact there are many more job opportunities in Calgary, but it is cheaper to live in Airdrie.

In 2018, Airdrie created a new economic strategy which will meet its goal by 2028. Made with the community input of residents and business owners, they created three key objectives: drawing visitors and entrepreneurs to Airdrie, capitalizing from Airdrie's benefits such as its location and technologies, and attracting new businesses while sustaining the current ones.

In addition, Airdrie is investing to continue to support their growth. They have received a 36% increase in employment between 2016 to 2021 and are investing 132 million dollars in road improvement projects.
