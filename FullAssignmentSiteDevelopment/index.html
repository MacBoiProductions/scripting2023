About Airdrie

Incorporated in 1909, Airdrie has always been steadily growing since its inception as a village. Named after the town of the same name over in Scotland, Airdrie has become one of the fastest growing cities in the past few decades, having grown from a village to a small town to a city. It is located on a lush prairie with big, open fields. With easygoing citizens and local attractions incorporating the city's history, everyone should feel right at home when visiting Airdrie!

Many new opportunities are arising as more people move to the city. As new real estate continues to be built, the city is expanding to incorporate more people.

Since 1997, Airdrie has also partnered with and become the sister city of Gwacheon, located in South Korea.
